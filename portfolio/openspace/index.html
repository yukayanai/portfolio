Open Colleges

As a designer in Open Colleges, I was responsible for implementing interface and visual design for the learning platform to create the best learning experiences for the students.

OpenSpace is an online campus for students to access all their learning materials including videos and digital resources, assessments, grades and feedback. Through OpenSpace, students can also connect with their Trainers, Assessors, Program Managers, online support network and other students.
